The Process

In a fast paced, mobile first world every business or organization needs an accessible and responsive website to give you a competitive edge. My goal is to give you a website that is responsive easy to access and elegant to enable you inteact with your clients.

Brain Storming: You have an idea for website or web app or software. Then lets talk about. We will talk together to go over the details, you show me what you want and need and we discuss how we can get there together

Design: I will create a detailed project proposal for you, along with visual aids to give you an idea of what your finished product will look like. From there we can fine tune the details and decide on the plan of action

Testing: Once we agree and I start work you will be updated according to the agreed upon timeline. You won't be left in the dark and we will use this time to test the project as we build.

Launching: This is the best part. Once project is done and we agree, then we launch. I am also available to provide ongoing support and updates as the need arises for the project
